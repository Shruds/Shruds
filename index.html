From Us To You

From Us To You

Hey guys!
It has been 5 crazy years! What a journey it has been. It can only get more eventful from here.
Looking back at our memories, it's amazing how far we have come from being the clueless college buddies to become partners for life. We met and found the meaning of life in each other. We then fell in love and went from just existing to living with a purpose. That wonderful feeling of being in love opened up an unknown dimension in our lives.
It's funny how it feels like we met just 5 minutes ago and yet also feels like we've known each other for an eternity.  Well, 'time' is weird, 'time' is relative, and the best 'time' is always now.
Certain things have no beginning while some have no endings. Love is like that. It's not the peak you conquer once, it's like a trek with an ever-changing landscape - endless yet beautiful.
We fell in love, and haven't stopped since then. It's like diving head first into a truly bottomless well. It's perfectly safe, trust us. ;)
But wait, us accepting each other is just step one. We believe that for a person living in a society, marriage is a 3-step process - in no particular order:

  • Acceptance from each other
  • Acceptance from parents and friends
  • Acceptance from the law
We’re on our way to complete the 3-step verification process. Love is already in the air but there can never be enough. Grace us with your presence and fill the air with more love, happiness and your generous blessings. 

Sudarshanan & Shrinidhi
